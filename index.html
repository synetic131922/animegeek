Anime's Impact on A Geek's Everyday Life

In a geek's life, anime has been his partner in life. It entertains him everyday, sometimes makes him cry, and sometimes makes him so amazed. Anime has made a huge impact on this fellow, and so much more geeks around the world. Having 4 seasons of anime is more than enough for a geek to fully satisfy him. This year may be a bit of a drag down, mainly because of the pandemic, but anime has never been this active and action-packed, and geeks all over the world are rejoicing.

Today, as a fellow anime geek who wants to express his love for anime, here are my favorites for each season of the year, as well as some recommendations and even some opening songs to enjoy. I may not be the best in judging how good an anime actually is, but I promise you, this will not disappoint! Hopefully, you enjoy reading my website!

My Preferences on Anime

My kind of anime is something that a geek like me wouldn't just enjoy, but would also take lessons and inspirations from the anime itself. My kind of anime are those shows that will make you impress, while also teaching you about the lessons in life that we geeks don't usually hear. I want a show that will motivate me so much to the point that I can become the main character in the story itself. As much as I am a big fan of fancy worlds and stunning visuals, I would choose the shows that will give you second thoughts on your decisions and overall, make you a fine geek ready to take on the world as we knew it.
